Core loop

Every research pass improves the base.

01

Ingest

Articles, papers, repos, datasets, images, and clips land in raw/ with their local assets intact.

02

Compile

The agent maintains wiki/: summaries, concept pages, backlinks, taxonomies, and index files.

03

Query

Questions trigger focused reading, synthesis, citations, and Obsidian-ready markdown or slide output.

04

File Back

Useful answers become durable wiki material instead of disappearing into a chat transcript.

Product shape

A compiler for personal research knowledge.

The product is not a replacement for Obsidian. It is an agent layer that treats the local filesystem as the durable source of truth and keeps the knowledge base coherent over time.

Vault Scanner

Tracks raw sources, derived pages, backlinks, stale files, and missing summaries.

Wiki Compiler

Creates concept pages, refreshes indexes, and keeps article relationships explicit.

Agent CLI

Lets the LLM search, inspect, render, and write outputs as repeatable commands.

Output Studio

Exports reports, Marp decks, graphs, matrices, and visual research artifacts.

Integrity layer

Health checks keep the wiki trustworthy.

Contradiction scan

Finds claims that disagree across sources and marks the page that needs review.

Missing evidence

Flags articles with weak citations, empty backlinks, or summaries without source coverage.

New article candidates

Suggests concepts that appear often enough to deserve their own durable page.

Output filing

Tracks which answers, charts, and slides should be promoted back into the wiki.
