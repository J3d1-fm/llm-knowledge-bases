# Competitor and Adjacent Product Notes

Adjacent categories include personal knowledge management, document chat, notebook assistants, note-taking tools, graph-based note tools, read-it-later workflows, and research managers.

The important product question is not whether another tool stores notes. The important question is whether the system treats the LLM as the maintainer of a durable filesystem wiki with source coverage, health checks, and reusable outputs.

This file needs source-specific expansion. Future imports should add individual raw notes for each tool, with exact product claims, URLs, dates accessed, and screenshots when useful.
